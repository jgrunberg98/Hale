Our Approach

We look at the full picture

Income. Taxes. Healthcare. Legacy. Every piece of your financial life works together — your plan should too.

Let's Talk

The transition from saving to spending is one of the biggest shifts in your financial life. We help you turn your 401(k), IRA, and other savings into a reliable paycheck that lasts through retirement.

  • 401(k) and IRA rollovers — Move your employer plan into an account you control, often with better options and lower fees.
  • Pension optimization — Choose the payout option that gives you and your spouse the most security.
  • Income strategies — Build a plan that covers your essential expenses first, then funds the retirement you want.

If you're approaching or already in retirement, protecting your principal is just as important as growing it. We specialize in products that keep your money safe while still earning competitive returns.

  • Fixed Index Annuities (FIA) — Your money earns interest tied to a market index, but you never lose principal when the market drops. Think of it as growth with a safety net.
  • Multi-Year Guaranteed Annuities (MYGA) — Like a CD from an insurance company, but often with higher interest rates. Your rate is locked in for a set number of years.
  • Single Premium Immediate Annuities (SPIA) — Convert a lump sum into guaranteed monthly income that starts right away — a personal pension.

Taxes can be one of the biggest expenses in retirement — often bigger than people expect. We help you keep more of what you've earned with smart withdrawal and conversion strategies.

  • Tax-efficient withdrawals — Know which accounts to draw from first (and when) to minimize your overall tax bill year after year.
  • Roth conversions — Move money from a traditional IRA to a Roth IRA at the right time, so future withdrawals are tax-free.
  • Tax-free retirement income — Structure your plan so a portion of your income doesn't show up on your tax return at all.

When you claim Social Security can mean a difference of tens of thousands of dollars over your lifetime. We analyze your situation to find the best strategy for you and your spouse.

  • Timing your benefits — Claiming at 62, 67, or 70 each has trade-offs. We'll show you which timing puts the most money in your pocket.
  • Spousal strategies — Coordinate benefits between you and your spouse to maximize your combined lifetime income.

Healthcare is one of the biggest unknowns in retirement. We help you navigate Medicare options and plan for long-term care costs before they become an emergency.

  • Medicare supplement selection — Original Medicare doesn't cover everything. We help you choose the right supplement (Medigap) plan to fill the gaps without overpaying.
  • Long-term care insurance — Protect your savings from the high cost of assisted living, nursing homes, or in-home care. We'll show you options that fit your budget and goals.

You've spent a lifetime building wealth. We help make sure it goes where you want it to — to the people and causes that matter most to you.

  • Wealth transfer strategies — Pass assets to your heirs in the most tax-efficient way possible, whether through trusts, gifting, or insurance.
  • Beneficiary planning — Make sure your accounts, policies, and assets are titled correctly so nothing falls through the cracks.

Insurance isn't just about protection — the right policy can also be a powerful planning tool for retirement income, tax savings, and legacy goals.

  • Guaranteed Universal Life (GUL) — Permanent life insurance with a guaranteed death benefit at a fixed premium. A straightforward way to leave a legacy or cover final expenses.
  • Indexed Universal Life (IUL) — A flexible policy that builds cash value tied to a market index. Can be used for tax-free retirement income or as a supplement to your other savings.
